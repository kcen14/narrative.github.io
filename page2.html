2. Fuel Efficiency based on Engine Cylinders

The scatterplot is logarithmically scaled. As can be seen, city MPGs are mostly lower than highway MPGs, with most car makes clustered between 10-50mpg, few makes in the middle,
and then fewer makes with high city and highway MPGs. You can mouseover each of the plotted data points to see details such as make and number of engine cylinders.
